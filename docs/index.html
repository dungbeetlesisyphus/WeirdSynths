Module Suite

Four modules. One face.

NERVE
20 HP · Face → CV
468 face landmarks become CV voltages. Eyebrow raise controls filter cutoff. Jaw opening modulates amplitude. Head tilt pans stereo. Your face is your modulation source.
Controller External Polyphonic
SKULL
12 HP · Face → Drums
Your face is a drum machine. Blink for kicks. Open your jaw for snares. Raise eyebrows for hi-hats. Built-in percussion synthesis with analog-modeled sounds.
Drum Controller External
MIRROR
14 HP · Face → Display
See yourself rendered in green phosphor. Dot-matrix CRT display shows your face as a wireframe, updating in real-time from the same tracking data driving NERVE and SKULL.
Visual External Utility
VOICE
8 HP · Audio → CV
Sing a note, get a voltage. YIN pitch detection outputs V/Oct to drive any oscillator. Plus envelope, onset triggers, spectral brightness, and polyphonic harmonics.

Sub-frame latency
UDP transport delivers face data to VCV Rack in under 10ms. Fast enough for real-time performance — you feel the connection between your face and the sound.
468 landmarks
Google MediaPipe's full face mesh gives you eyes, brows, jaw, mouth, nose, and head rotation. Each region becomes a distinct CV source in NERVE.
Works with any mic
VOICE doesn't need the bridge — it analyzes audio directly through VCV's audio input. Any microphone, instrument, or audio source works as a pitch controller.
Open source (GPL-3.0)
Every line of code is on GitHub. Fork it, mod it, learn from it. The bridge is MIT-licensed Python you can adapt for your own face-controlled projects.
